The Author

Rory Stouder

Rory Stouder is a technology entrepreneur and science fiction author based in Indiana. A lifelong fan of military sci-fi, space opera, and westerns, he spent years building companies in the tech space before turning his imagination loose on the page.

Ranger Group 42: Deadman's Gulch is his debut novel ‚Äî a story that blends the grit of military fiction with the vast possibilities of speculative science. Drawing on themes of duty, sacrifice, corporate overreach, and the cost of progress, the book reflects both his love for the genre and his perspective as someone who builds things for a living.

When he's not writing or running Gain3d Studios, Rory can be found exploring new tech, debating starship designs, or planning the next chapter of the Ranger Group 42 saga.
